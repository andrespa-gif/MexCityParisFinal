# CDMX HDI methodology

Human Development Index values per alcaldía come from PNUD Mexico municipal HDI publications, mapped to decades 1990, 2000, 2010, 2015, and 2020 in `urban-index-data.json`.

Source: PNUD *Informe sobre Desarrollo Humano Municipal en México* / municipal IDH tables for Ciudad de México.

These are published values, not recomputed locally. The scatterplot and HDI map use the nearest available HDI year for each selected UQI decade.
